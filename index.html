Gray Matter Ops · Duke Design Defense Studio 2026

Turning survival
hours into days.

X49 is a localized, opioid-free nanofiber designed to be placed in the field, even by a non-medic, and to control pain at the wound for days. It keeps the wounded conscious, mobile, and survivable until evacuation. Developed with Dr. Matthew Becker and Aminyx Therapeutics.
