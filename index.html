Gray Matter Ops · Duke Design Defense Studio 2026

Turning survival
hours into days.

SYMMVIA is a localized, opioid-free nanofiber packed straight into the wound. One application holds pain at the injury for days, without sedating the casualty and with no way to overdose. It fits a standard IFAK, takes no needle and no removal, and bioresorbs as the body heals. In published Duke research it outperformed the same drug given by injection for three days running. Developed by Dr. Matthew Becker and Aminyx Therapeutics; Gray Matter Ops is the Duke Design Defense Studio team working to get it to the field.
